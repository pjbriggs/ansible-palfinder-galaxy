Acknowledging use of the Mintaka Galaxy Service

Please reference both the BCF and University of Manchester Research IT Services in any research journal or publication that requires citation of any author's work.

The recognition of BCF and IT Services support is important to ensure the sustainability of the service.

The minimal content of the citation should include:

"The authors would like to acknowledge the use of the Mintaka Galaxy service provided by the Bioinformatics Core Facility and IT Services at the University of Manchester."

In addition:

  • The documentation of any specific Galaxy tools you used will also list citation information, if relevant, and
  • Please see https://wiki.galaxyproject.org/CitingGalaxy for general information on citing use of the Galaxy platform

Finally: please let us know when you publish so we can add you to our publications list.
